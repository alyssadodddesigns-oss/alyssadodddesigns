About Alyssa Dodd Designs

'Siyo (Hello)! My name is Alyssa Dodd and I am a Graphic Designer. Alyssa Dodd Designs is my personal website that I created to promote my graphic design work. I'd love the opportunity to help you and your buisness grow. It's my passion to make unique, meaningful design work that companies, businesses, and individuals can feel proud of!

Skills

My skills include Photoshop, Illustrator, In Design, Acrobat, Dream Weaver, HTML coding, and website design (I coded this website)!

Education and Employment

I am an undergraduate student, majoring in Graphic Design at Southeastern Oklahoma State University. I had the opportunity to intern during my undergraduate at Black Hawk Signs in Coalgate, Oklahoma. During this time, I created several sign designs, logos, and banners for various buisnesses in the area. I continue to further my design skills in university as I work towards my bachelors!
